### Key Observations

1. **Jamyang Sherab (transfer) is the only trainee with visual/media production experience.** Documentary film, photography, content creation — strongest Design candidate. Everyone else in Design starts from zero.
2. **9 IT/CS people are your strongest asset.** They can ramp faster on Dev and potentially help peers.
3. **62% are unemployed.** High motivation expected — this is a real career opportunity for them.
4. **Only 29% have prior Desuung training.** Most are experiencing structured/disciplined training for the first time through Cohort 3's Desuung phase.
5. **JNEC (Jigme Namgyel Engineering College) is heavily represented** — 9 trainees. Consider this for peer support groupings.
6. **Week 1 aptitude assessment is the real sorting mechanism.** Academic background is a signal, not a verdict. Some arts grads may excel at testing or dev, some engineers may prefer BA.
