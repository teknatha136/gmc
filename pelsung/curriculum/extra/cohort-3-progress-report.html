Essential Services Registry for Gelephu Mindfulness City

ZorigHub

An online marketplace for Bhutanese artisans

An e-commerce-style platform that lets Bhutanese artists register their craft and lets buyers browse and purchase products. ZorigHub gives traditional artisans a modern storefront — surfacing local craftsmanship to a national and international audience while keeping registration, listing, and purchase flows simple enough for non-technical sellers. Inspired by a project idea carried over from a previous Pelsung cohort, now picked up and built out by Cohort 3.

Project · 042 teams

Migsel — GMC Resonance Portal

Citizen-to-city reporting for utilities and public services

A proactive digital response to the daily frustrations of residents — overflowing trash bins, dark streetlights, choked drainage, crumbling roads — that often go ignored. Migsel pairs a live map of waste centers with a simple, direct way to report utility failures, closing the gap between community needs and city action. The aim is to convert reactive maintenance into a data-driven, accountable partnership between residents and GMC services — turning every neighbor into a participant in keeping their streets healthy, safe, and clean.
