## How to Pick

When you go through this, ask for each candidate:

1. **Does GMCA / Pelsung / SELISE Bhutan have someone who would actually use it next week?** If no real user, skip.
2. **Can we point to a specific person who will say "yes, I need this"?** That's your sponsor.
3. **Is the data already available, or does it need to be created?** Avoid projects that need months of data collection before they're useful.
4. **Is it boring enough to ship?** Excitement is suspicious — it usually means the team will over-engineer.

Once you mark your favorites, we can run them through deeper scoping (stakeholders, data sources, MVP definition, group composition).
