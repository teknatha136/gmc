   - More focused team sizes

4. **Gaps neither cohort fills well:**
   - No education/learning management software
   - No citizen-facing government services portal
   - No communication/collaboration tools
   - No data analytics / BI tools for GMC operations
   - No cybersecurity or identity management (beyond MSCP)
